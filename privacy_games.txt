Qubics Integrated Learning- Privacy Policy
-----------------------
In category of games and entertainment apps we do not collect and share any personal data from user. However the advertisers that we use may collect and process personal data for the purpose of personalizing advertisement experience.

In category of education apps user mobile number or email id may be used for login. But this information is used for login purpose only and not shared with any entity.

Android permissions
-------------------
Following Android permissions are used by advertising networks
android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE
android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE

Advertisers
-----------
    Gaming apps use ad network provider Google Admob to display advertisements.
